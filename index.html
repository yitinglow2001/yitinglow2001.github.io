About Us

M Florist is a family-owned and operated business that has been serving the community for over 20 years. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the freshest and most beautiful flowers, as well as exceptional customer service. Our team of experienced florists takes pride in creating stunning floral arrangements for all occasions, including weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and more.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to meet all of your floral needs. Our services include:

  • Custom floral arrangements for all occasions
  • Wedding and event floral design
  • Flower delivery
  • Corporate and business floral services
  • Funeral and sympathy arrangements

Contact us today to learn more about our services or to schedule a consultation with one of our expert florists.
